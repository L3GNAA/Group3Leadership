What Are Leadership Behaviors?

Leadership behaviors are the actions and attitudes that inspire, guide, and support others toward shared goals. They include showing integrity, respect, care, and adaptability, not just giving directions, but creating an environment where others can do their best work.

Leadership isn't limited to people with titles or authority. Anyone can lead by example, by communicating clearly, taking initiative, helping teammates overcome challenges, and keeping the group focused on success.

In short: Leadership behaviors are about how you show up, motivating, supporting, and guiding others through your actions, not just your role.
